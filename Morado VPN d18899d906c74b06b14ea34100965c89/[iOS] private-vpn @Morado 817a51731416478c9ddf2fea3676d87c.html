[iOS] private-vpn @Morado

1- Open the App Store and download the OpenVPN Connect app

You must connect your iPhone to the computer and from iTunes or Finder. When you select your device you will find “Share Files”, select the OpenVPN App

Download the file vpn-windows.zip in your computer

Unzip the downloaded file to a folder of your choice.

In the box on the right side of iTunes you can drag the files that you just unzipped and that you have saved on your computer. (select all downloaded files (ca-Morado, Morado.key, Morado, Morado.ovpn)

Press the “Sync” button and disconnect your iPhone from the computer

Enter the OpenVPN application and select the ADD option

Write your Username and select “Save password” immediately the option to write your VPN password will be enabled, when trying to connect you must allow the application to add VPN configurations to your iPhone.

You will already be connected to the VPN and you will be able to use the services that require a secure connection.
